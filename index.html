The Band

Formerly with Blues Image, Mike has performed with many other well known groups. Along with his vocals, he brings additional rhythm to the group

Eric is a veteran of Iron Butterfly and has travelled the world with the original band beginning in 1995. He returns with his lead vocals, powerful guitar playing and distinct sound. Eric has performed with many other well known artists. Eric is known as a world class guitarist and vocalist.

Dave returns again to Iron Butterfly providing the heavy bass lines. Dave is best known as the bass player for the rock band Spock's Beard. He has also played with such artists as Eric Burdon, Bobby Kimball of Toto, Mark Lindsay of Paul Revere & The Raiders!

Ray was a longtime member of Wishbone Ash and joined the house bands for Viva Cabaret with Tom Jones. He returned to Wishbone Ash from 1997 - 2007. In 2010 Ray toured 14 countries with Iron Butterfly.

German violinist, keyboardist and composer Martin Gerschwitz, who had formelly worked with Lita Ford, Meat Loaf, Walter Trout and Eric Burdon and The Animals, joined the band in 2005, replacing Larry Rust. He stayed until 2012. He has returned to bring his immense talent back to Iron Butterfly.

About the band

The Los Angeles music climate of the late sixties and seventies was characterized by the heavy sounds and sentiments that reflected the revolutionary attitudes of the generation. Bursting onto the scene was Iron Butterfly, whose sound not only epitomized the hard attitudes of the youth with its heavy drumming and bass lines, but also embraced more delicate aspirations through its intricate guitar and keyboard overlays.
